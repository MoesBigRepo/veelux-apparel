Priority Fixes

impact order
01

Add Product and Organization JSON-LD

The single biggest visibility unlock. The theme is suppressing Shopify's default schema emission; until restored, rich results are impossible. Product, AggregateOffer, Organization and BreadcrumbList on their respective templates.

02

Remove or replace the broken locale selector

Kills the 571 dead links and the bulk of the DOM bloat in a single theme edit. If multi-currency is wanted later, wire a functioning selector.

03

Rewrite the homepage title and meta

Brand plus category plus positioning. Example: "VEELUX — Luxury Rhinestone Streetwear | Brooklyn Limited Drops."

04

Finish the About page

Remove the literal "Sub Title" and "Heading" placeholder strings and publish the full brand story already drafted for the product line.

05

Responsive images and alt repair

Shopify's native image_tag with width sets provides srcset for free. Replace alt="index" with a descriptive sentence and fill the nine empty alts.

Access note. Every fix above requires Shopify admin or theme access, which is not currently on record. These findings are packaged for hand-off to the store owner rather than direct implementation.
