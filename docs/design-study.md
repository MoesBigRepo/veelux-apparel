## Convergent patterns (the playbook)
1. **Image-first, full-bleed hero** — the imagery is the design; text is minimal and overlaid.
2. **Centered, tiny, wide-tracked uppercase wordmark**; nav reduced to the minimum (spread links or hamburger).
3. **Monochrome restraint** — one near-black + one paper tone; a single small accent pop. Product carries the color.
4. **Generous whitespace** + large type-scale contrast (tiny labels ↔ big display).
5. **Sparse captions** — product name + price, nothing else, on a quiet grid.
6. **Full-figure model imagery** in multi-up rows (not cropped flatlays).
7. **Neo-grotesque sans** for everything, often a **mono** accent for labels/prices (Gallery Dept) = utilitarian streetwear signal.
8. Refined micro-CTAs (thin outline, uppercase, tracked).

## Translation to Veelux (dark-luxe inversion)
Veelux = Brooklyn rhinestone streetwear, black hero, "All Eyes on You." So we **invert to dark**
(differentiates from the mostly-white peers, flatters rhinestone sparkle and their black/pink line):

- **Palette**: obsidian `#0a0a0b` base, bone `#f4f1ea` text, with a restrained **iridescent/silver rhinestone shimmer** accent for hairlines/hovers, and a soft **pink** secondary (nods to the women's line). One accent, used sparingly.
- **Type**: `Archivo` (expanded grotesque) for the wordmark + display; `Inter` for body/UI; a **monospace** for prices/labels/SKU (Gallery-Dept utilitarian cue).
- **Layout**: centered wide-tracked `VEELUX` wordmark, minimal nav. Full-bleed hero (Seedance video) with a single "All Eyes on You" line + thin outline CTA. Quiet product grid, full-figure imagery, name+price mono captions. Big whitespace, hairline dividers.
- **Motion** (Framer): scroll-reveal on sections, staggered grid entrance, hero parallax/scale, sticky PDP gallery, page transitions, a subtle rhinestone shimmer sweep on the wordmark/hero.
