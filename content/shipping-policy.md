Shipping policy

Thank you for shopping with   Vee Lux Apparel ! We strive to provide you with a seamless shopping experience from start to finish. Below you will find our shipping and delivery information to guide you through the process:

1. Shipping Options:   We offer several shipping options to accommodate your needs:

Standard Shipping: Estimated delivery within 5-7 business days.

Express Shipping: Estimated delivery within 2-3 business days.

International Shipping: Delivery times may vary depending on the destination.

2. Processing Time:   Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Please note that processing times may be slightly extended during peak seasons or promotional periods.

3. Shipping Rates:   Shipping rates are calculated based on the weight of your order and your chosen shipping method. You can view the shipping costs at checkout before completing your purchase.

4. Order Tracking:   Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking information. You can track the status of your order using the provided tracking number.

5. Delivery Time-frames:   Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected. While we make every effort to ensure timely delivery, please note that unforeseen circumstances such as weather delays or carrier issues may affect delivery times.

6. Shipping Restrictions:   Please be aware that some items may be subject to shipping restrictions due to size, weight, or destination. We will notify you if any restrictions apply to your order.

7. International Shipping:   For international orders, please be aware that customs duties, taxes, and fees may apply upon delivery. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in the shipping cost.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding shipping and delivery, please feel free to contact our customer support team at support@veeluxapparel.com
